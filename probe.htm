Prohibited behaviours

  1. Category 1 (C1)
    • techniques which make excessive contact, having regard to the scoring area attacked, and techniques which make contact with the throat.
    • Attacks to arm or leg, groin, joints or instep.
    • Attacks to the face with open hand techniques.
    • Dangerous or forbidden throwing techniques.
  2. Category 2 (C2)
    • Feigning or exaggerating injury.
    • Exit from the competition area, not caused by the opponent.
    • Avoiding combat as a means of preventing the opponent to get an opportunity to attack.
    • Grabbimg the opponent with both hands for any other than executing a takedown.
    • For the safety of the opponent, c2 is given to the player for performing any technique, which by their nature cannot be controlled.
    • Failing to obey oders of the refree and showing discourteous behaviours.
    • Clinching, wrestling, pushing or standing chest to chest without attemting any scoring attack.
  3. Chukoku
    • It is imposed on the player for the first instance of a minor infraction.
  4. Keikoku
    • It is imposed on the player for the second instance of a minor infraction for that category or for the infractions not sufficient to merit HANSOKU-CHUI
  5. Hansoku-chui
    • This is a warning of disqualification, imposed on infraction done after getting Keikoku, or on a serious infraction.
  6. Hansoku
    • It is given to disqualify a player if an infraction is done after getting Hansoku-chui.
